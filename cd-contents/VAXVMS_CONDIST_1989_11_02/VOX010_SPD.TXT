
PRODUCT NAME: DECvoice Software, Version 1.0		    SPD 29.97.00

DESCRIPTION

DECvoice is a soft-loadable, multi-function voice module containing
subsystems for performing text-to-speech synthesis, isolated word 
recognition, digitized voice record and playback, and telephone control.

The text-to-speech subsystem supports functionality comparable to 
that supported by the DECtalk speech synthesizer.  The digitized
speech subsystem supports record/playback for the following
encoding algorithms; 64k bit/sec uLAW, 64k bits/sec ALAW, 
16k bits/sec sub band encoding. The telephone processing subsystem 
fully complies with US and Canadian regulations.
